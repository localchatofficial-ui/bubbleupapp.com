Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about nearby chat rooms, privacy, and location-based discovery.

Can I use BubbleUp anonymously?

Yes. You can join many rooms without creating a profile. Create an account if you want to manage rooms and preferences.

How does location privacy work?

BubbleUp uses approximate location context for room discovery. Your exact location is not shared with other users.

What can rooms be used for?

Rooms work well for neighborhood updates, local events, study groups, city tips, and live area-based discussions.
