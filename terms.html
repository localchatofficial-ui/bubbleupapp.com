4. How BubbleUp Works

Location-Based Discovery

  • Rooms are tied to geographic locations
  • You can discover and join rooms near your current location
  • Location permission is required for the app to function

Room Location Options

  • My Location (GPS) - Creates a room near your current location. Your exact position is converted to a Geohash (~5km area) on your device before being sent to our servers, protecting your precise location.
  • Custom Location - Select any area on the map. The room will appear at the center of the nearest ~1km Geohash cell.

Ephemeral Rooms

  • Rooms automatically expire after a set duration
  • Free Tier Duration: 1-6 hours
  • Pro Duration: Up to 7 days
  • When a room expires, it becomes inaccessible to all users
  • Room creators can close rooms early
