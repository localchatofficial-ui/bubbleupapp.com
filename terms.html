4. How BubbleUp Works

Location-Based Discovery

  • Rooms are tied to geographic locations
  • You can discover and join rooms near your current location
  • Location permission is required for the app to function

Room Location Options

  • My Location (GPS) - Uses your current location, rounded to the nearest ~1km grid to protect your exact position
  • Custom Location - Select any area on the map; the room will appear at the center of the nearest ~1km grid to ensure privacy

Ephemeral Rooms

  • Rooms automatically expire after 1-24 hours (set by the creator)
  • When a room expires, it becomes inaccessible to all users
  • Room creators can close rooms early

Room Categories
